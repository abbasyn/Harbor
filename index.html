Optimizing the cash conversion cycle

Calculate Your Working Capital Efficiency

The cash conversion cycle is a metric that indicates the operational efficiency of a business. Calculated by adding inventory days and days sales outstanding, both assets, minus the amount of days payables outstanding, a liability, it measures how long input is tied up in production and sales before it is converted into cash received.
